The Sacred Badrinath Pilgrimage

Badrinath is one of the four sites in the Char Dham pilgrimage and one of the 108 Divya Desams. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu in the form of Badrinarayan. Situated at 3,133 meters, it is one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in India.

Visit the temple, explore Mana Village - the last Indian village before Tibet, and experience the divine energy of this sacred place.
