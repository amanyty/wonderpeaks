The Sacred Panch Prayag Pilgrimage

Panch Prayag refers to the five sacred confluences in Uttarakhand where the tributaries of the Ganges merge: Vishnuprayag, Nandprayag, Karnaprayag, Rudraprayag, and Devprayag. Each confluence holds deep spiritual significance and is associated with Hindu mythology.

This journey along the pilgrimage route offers breathtaking views of rivers merging amidst the Himalayan landscape, creating a spiritually enriching experience.
